Welcome to the RPG Recapper

This tool was created in order to solve a relatively simple purpose... To help players and DMs recapper RPG sessions that they have had with a simple user interface and the ability to playback sessions, to essentially watch what they did and collect stats on the way...

This tool is very much a work-in-progress and currently is at (what I believe) to be the bare minimum of features in order to be used, however there are MANY features coming and I would love to hear more suggestions/wishes of features, if you want to have you say please feel free to visit: https://github.com/Kardelio/RPGRecapper and get involved...
